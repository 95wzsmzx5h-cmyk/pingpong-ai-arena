Privacy Policy — Ping Pong AI Arena

Effective date: [DATE]

Ping Pong AI Arena is a BYOK Chrome extension that runs AI-vs-AI debates using user-provided API keys.

What we collect

  • No accounts required.
  • We do not collect browsing history, page content, cookies, or website traffic.
  • We store locally on your device: your API key (if entered) and your settings/preferences.

How we use data

  • Your API key is used only to send requests directly to the AI provider endpoints you configure.

Storage

  • Keys and settings are stored locally using chrome.storage.local.
  • No developer-owned prompt relay servers are used.

Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Prompts are sent only to the AI provider you choose; their policies govern their handling.

Deletion

  • Delete your key/settings using Clear/Reset in the extension Settings.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes local extension storage.
